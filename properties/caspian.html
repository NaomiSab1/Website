About CASPIAN

Graceful proportion
in a prestige address.

Positioned in one of Ascot's most prestigious and sought-after addresses, CASPIAN is a residence of exceptional grace and refined architectural proportion. Named for the world's largest enclosed body of water — a landscape of vast, tranquil beauty — CASPIAN evokes a sense of generous space and serene grandeur that is immediately apparent upon arrival.

The home's foyer is a statement of intent: double-height ceiling, honed limestone floors, and a custom steel-and-glass staircase that immediately communicates the calibre of what lies within. The interior architecture is characterised by a sophisticated restraint — each space pared back to its essential elements, allowing the quality of materials and craftsmanship to speak without distraction.

The kitchen is a culinary theatre of the highest order: integrated Gaggenau appliances, a waterfall-edge stone island of impressive proportion, and custom joinery of extraordinary quality. Five bedrooms, each with a thoughtfully designed ensuite, culminate in a master retreat that is nothing short of a private hotel: a dressing room of walk-in proportion, a king suite that opens to a private terrace, and a spa ensuite finished entirely in Calacatta marble.
