About SOLACE

A sanctuary of
calm and beauty.

SOLACE is an exercise in architectural restraint and material refinement. Positioned in a quiet, tree-lined street of Camp Hill — one of inner Brisbane's most beloved family suburbs — this extraordinary five-bedroom home embodies the Sabdia philosophy of creating residences that are simultaneously beautiful and deeply liveable.

The name SOLACE speaks precisely to the feeling evoked by the home itself: a profound sense of calm and well-being that settles over you the moment you cross the threshold. Conceived as a sanctuary from the world outside, SOLACE features flowing open-plan living spaces that dissolve effortlessly to a north-facing terrace and resort-style pool — an indoor-outdoor relationship of exceptional generosity.

The kitchen is a culinary architect's dream: vast stone benchtops, custom joinery, premium appliances, and a butler's pantry of generous proportion. Four bedrooms — each with ensuite — surround a master suite of genuine grandeur, complete with a walk-in robe and spa ensuite that rivals the finest hotel suites. A four-car garage and discreet storage throughout ensure that this home accommodates the demands of family life without the slightest compromise to its architectural elegance.
